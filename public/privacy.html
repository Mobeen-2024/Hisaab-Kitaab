Hisaib Kitaib Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Hisaib Kitaib. Your privacy and the security of your financial data are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Hisaib Kitaib stores your data locally on your device by default.

1. Data Storage & Cloud Sync

  • All your financial records, transactions, customers, and inventory are stored locally on your own device.
  • Cloud Sync is optional. If enabled, your data is synced using Firebase (Google Cloud) to allow access across multiple devices and prevent data loss.

2. AI, Voice, and OCR Features

  • AI, Voice, and OCR features are optional.
  • If you choose to use these features (such as voice entry, AI assistant, or receipt scanning), the related text, audio, or images may be sent to Google Gemini for processing.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell user data.
  • We do not share your personal or financial information with any third-party advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Backups

Important: Backup files generated by the app contain your personal financial records. Please keep them private and store them securely.

5. Data Deletion & Contact

You can delete all your local data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Factory Reset" option within the app settings.
